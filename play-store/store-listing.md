## Full description (max 4000 characters)

```
Leanster turns your phone into a motorcycle telemetry dashboard. Mount it, calibrate once, and every ride becomes data you can actually learn from — lean angle, pitch, speed, GPS route and corner-by-corner breakdowns.

Built for riders who want to understand their cornering, not just record a track.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
WHAT IT MEASURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Real-time lean angle (left & right) with sensor fusion for smooth, accurate readings
• Pitch angle for braking and acceleration
• Speed and distance via GPS — or a connected external USB GPS module for higher precision
• Automatic corner detection with entry/exit lean and direction

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
AFTER THE RIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Full session summary: max lean, top speed, distance, corner count and more
• Corner breakdown with a map of every turn you took
• Lean symmetry and a performance report card
• Ghost replay — watch your ride play back on a canvas timeline
• Place-named sessions so your history reads like a logbook

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
MAKE IT YOURS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Clean "Carbon & Neon" dashboard designed to be readable at a glance
• Portrait or landscape — pick your default mounting orientation
• Dynamic brand icons for your marque
• Optional voice coach with real-time audio cues

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
• Everything is stored on your device — no account, no sign-up, no ads
• No analytics or tracking SDKs
• Export any ride to CSV, or share a clean ride card image — only when you choose to

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
IMPORTANT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Leanster is a training and data-analysis tool. Readings are estimates and depend on correct calibration and mounting. It is NOT certified instrumentation. Never interact with the app while riding — set it up before you go, review your data when stopped. Always ride within your limits and obey local laws.

Ride safely. Ride smarter. Lean in.
```
(~1,850 characters — comfortably under the 4000 limit)
