# Content Rating & Target Audience — Google Play Console

App: **Leanster** (`com.ridetracker.ride`)
Location in console: *App content → Content ratings* and *App content → Target audience and content*

> The content-rating questionnaire (run by IARC) generates your age ratings automatically from your answers. Below are the correct answers for Leanster, plus the target-audience settings.

---

## Category

- **App category:** Choose **App** (not Game). Suggested Play category: *Maps & Navigation* or *Auto & Vehicles*.

## Content rating questionnaire answers

Answer **No** to essentially everything — Leanster is a data-logging utility with no objectionable content:

| Question area | Answer |
|---------------|--------|
| Violence (cartoon, fantasy, realistic) | No |
| Blood / gore | No |
| Sexual or suggestive content, nudity | No |
| Profanity / crude humor | No |
| Drugs, alcohol, tobacco references | No |
| Gambling (simulated or real) | No |
| Scary / horror content | No |
| User-generated content / user interaction / chat | No (rides are shared only via the OS share sheet; there is no in-app social feed or messaging) |
| Shares user's location with other users | **No** (location is on-device; the user chooses to share exported files, but the app has no feature that broadcasts location to other users) |
| In-app purchases / paid digital content | No (donation links are external Ko-fi/PayPal, not IAP) |
| Contains ads | No |

**Expected result:** rated suitable for everyone / PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone.

> Honesty note: Motorcycling is a real-world risky activity, but the *app content* is non-objectionable. The questionnaire rates content, not the underlying hobby. Your in-app safety disclaimer already addresses responsible-riding messaging.

## Target audience and content

- **Target age groups:** select adult ranges (e.g. **18+**, or 18 and 16–17 if you wish). **Do not** include any under-13 group — that would trigger Play Families requirements you don't want.
- **Appeals to children?** → **No**.
- **Google Play Families Policy / "Designed for Families":** → **Not** enrolled.

## Ads declaration

- **Does your app contain ads?** → **No**.

## Notes

- If you later add any in-app social sharing feed, in-app purchases, or ads, you must re-run this questionnaire and update these answers.
